This is me

As a highly motivated and talented biologist, I discovered my passion for data science through my first biostatistics courses. My commitment to learning led me to master the programming language R, which is specifically designed for statistical analysis. I then went on to pursue a doctorate in quantitative ecology where I honed my skills in GIS and basic database concepts. My thirst for knowledge and experience took me on research internships in various countries including Canada, the United States, the Czech Republic, and Italy. These opportunities allowed me to meet and learn from individuals who were deeply passionate about data, inspiring me to constantly strive for excellence in my field.

To further my skills, I made a conscious effort to learn new programming languages such as Python and SQL and became well-versed in the importance of data visualization. My passion for data science led me to pursue a master's degree in this field, further solidifying my expertise and elevating me to a new level of proficiency.

My strong background in biology, combined with my extensive knowledge in data science and programming, has allowed me to excel in my current role as a data analyst. I am confident in my ability to turn data into meaningful insights and drive impactful decisions, making me a valuable asset to any organization. I am always eager to take on new challenges and continuously grow my skills and knowledge in the field of data science.
